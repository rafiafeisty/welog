WeLog Blogging Application

The Welog provides a comprehensive platform for users to engage with blog content. At its core, it offers user authentication, allowing new users to sign up with their details, log in to access personalized features, and log out securely. Once authenticated, users gain access to robust blog management tools; they can create new blog posts complete with titles, descriptions, and images, and also edit or delete their own published content. All visitors, including those who are not registered, can view all blog posts. Beyond content creation, the application fosters interaction by enabling registered users to leave comments on posts and express their appreciation through likes and dislikes. Each user also has a dedicated profile that showcases their published blogs and overall activity on the platform.

To experience the thrill of blogging and exploring others' blogs, make an account on the website and register yourself to enjoy blogging and stay updated with recent trends.

If you are a registered user, log in to your homepage and enjoy blogging. View your own blogs, replies, likes, and place likes/comments on others' blogs. Have fun blogging!
